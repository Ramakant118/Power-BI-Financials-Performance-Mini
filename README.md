**Finance Performance Dashboard**

Project Overview

This project presents a Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to evaluate revenue, profit, customer distribution, and operational costs across multiple regions.

The dataset represents financial data from four regions over several months, including information on customers, marketing costs, operating costs, revenue, and profit.

The goal of this dashboard is to help businesses monitor financial performance, identify profitable products, and understand regional performance trends.

Dataset Description

The dataset includes financial and operational information with the following fields:

Customer
Date
Product
Region
Revenue
Profit
Marketing Cost
Operating Cost

This data allows analysis of profitability, cost management, and revenue growth across different products and regions.

 Business Questions Answered

The North region produces the highest profit compared to other regions.

2️. Which product is the most profitable?

The Loan product generates the highest profit among all products.

3️. How does revenue change over time?

Revenue shows a positive growth trend month over month.

4️. Which region performs best overall?

The North region shows strong performance in both revenue and profit.

The dashboard suggests that marketing spending may contribute to improved revenue performance, indicating the potential impact of marketing investments.
